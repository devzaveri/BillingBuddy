BillingBuddy Account Deletion

At BillingBuddy, we respect your data privacy rights. This page explains how you can delete your account and all associated data from our service.

How to Delete Your Account

Open the BillingBuddy app on your device.
Navigate to Settings by tapping on the profile icon in the bottom navigation bar.
Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" button.
Tap "Delete Account" and confirm your decision by entering your password when prompted.
Tap "Delete" to permanently delete your account and all associated data.
Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your personal information, groups, expenses, and other data will be permanently removed from our servers.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Groups you've created where you're the only member
  • Your expense records
  • Your authentication data

Need Help?

If you're having trouble deleting your account or have any questions about our data deletion policy, please contact us at support@billingbuddy.app.
